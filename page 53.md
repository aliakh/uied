**chip** **chipping chipped**
1 *(v)* to break a small piece off something by accident. **chip** *(n)*.
2 *(n)* a long, thin piece of potato, cooked in oil.
3 if you have a **chip on your shoulder**, you feel angry because you think you have been treated unfairly.
4 *(n)* *(US)* a very thin, flat piece of potato cooked in oil (crisp, UK).
5 *(n)* a minute piece of silicon, with electronic circuits printed on it, used in computers and electronic equipment.

**chivalry** *(shiv-ul-ree)*
1 *(n)* very polite and helpful behaviour, especially by a man towards a woman. **chivalrous** *(adj)*.

**chlorophyll** *(klor-oh-fill)* *(n)* the green substance in plants that allows them to use the Sun's energy.
