1 *(n)* a small, flat, round playing piece used in some board games.
2 *(n)* a long, flat surface. *A shop counter.*

**counteract** *counteracting counteracted* *(v)* to act against something so that it is less effective. *You should take some exercise to counteract the effects of overeating.*
