**go** *goes going went gone*
1. *(v)* to move away from or towards a place. *I'm going home.*
2. *(v)* to work properly. *This machine won't go.*
3. *(v)* to become. *The class went quiet.*
4. *(v)* If you are **going to** do something, you will do it in the future.
5. *(v)* to have a place, or to belong. *Where do the plates go?*
6. *(n)* a turn. *It's my go at batting.*
