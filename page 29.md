**bat** batting batted
1. *(n)* a small, flying mammal that comes out at night to feed. Bats find their way around by making high-pitched squeaks which send back echoes that are picked up by their sensitive ears.

3. *(v)* to take a turn at hitting the ball and scoring runs in games such as cricket and baseball. *It's Barry's turn to bat.* **batsman** *(n)*
