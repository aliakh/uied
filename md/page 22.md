2. *(n)* a piece of writing published in a newspaper or magazine.
3. *(n)* a word, such as "a", "the", or "some", that goes in front of a noun.

---

**artificial intelligence** *(n)* the use of computers to do things that previously needed human intelligence, such as understanding language.

**artillery**

**artist** *(n)* someone very skilled at painting, drawing, or making things. **artistic** *(adj)*, **artistically** *(adv)*
